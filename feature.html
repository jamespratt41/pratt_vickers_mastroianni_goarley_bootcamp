Overview

Last year, our company was tasked with redesigning the Thames River Anglers Association’s website.The TRAA works at Grassroots levelwithin the Thames River area and do most of their workprotecting species of fish and promoting environmental clean-up. They asked for a neater presentation of their content and an overall more appealing vibe to exemplify what they do and offer.

Goal and Result

The goal was to perform a full website re-design.We developed a fresh site with a vibrant appeal, re-arranged content, and took our own photo/ shot video of the beautiful Thames are to produce an overall clean user experience.
